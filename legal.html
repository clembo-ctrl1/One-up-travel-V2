1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytics about how the site is used.

2. Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function. These include authentication cookies (keeping you signed in) and security cookies. You cannot opt out of these.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with the platform. We use these to improve our Service. Data is aggregated and anonymised.

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings, such as preferred currency, language, and display preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services embedded in our platform (such as Google Maps for trip mapping, and booking partner widgets) may set their own cookies. These are governed by the respective third party's cookie policy.

3. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, blocking essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning properly.

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
