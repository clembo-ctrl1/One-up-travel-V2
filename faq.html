Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about One-Up Travel. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our team.

What is One-Up Travel?
One-Up Travel is an AI-powered travel planning platform. We use artificial intelligence to help you discover destinations, plan itineraries, find accommodation, and create unforgettable trips â€” all in one place.
Who is Lisa?
Lisa is our AI travel assistant. She can recommend destinations, suggest hotels and activities, build custom itineraries based on your preferences, budget, and travel style. Think of her as your personal travel agent, available 24/7.
Is One-Up Travel free to use?
Yes! One-Up Travel is currently free to use. You can create an account, chat with Lisa, and plan itineraries at no cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core travel planning will always remain accessible.
Can I book hotels and flights through One-Up Travel?
We're working on integrating direct booking capabilities. Currently, Lisa can recommend hotels and activities with real photos and reviews from Google, and direct you to the best places to book. Full booking integration is coming soon.
How accurate are Lisa's recommendations?
Lisa pulls real-time data from Google Places, including actual hotel photos, guest reviews, and ratings. However, prices and availability are estimates and should always be verified directly with the provider before booking. Always check visa requirements and travel advisories through official government sources.
How do I create an account?
Click "Sign In" in the top right corner and select "Create Account". You can sign up with your email and a password, or use Google Sign-In for one-click access. Your account lets you save itineraries and access them across devices.
How do I delete my account and data?
You can request account deletion by contacting us at Clem@one-up.cloud. We'll delete your account and all associated data within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for more details on your data rights.
Where is One-Up Travel based?
One-Up Travel is based in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. We're building a platform that helps travellers worldwide plan better trips using AI.
How can I contact the team?
You can reach us through our Contact page or email us directly at Clem@one-up.cloud. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

Still have questions?

We're constantly updating our FAQ. If you can't find what you're looking for, reach out and we'll help.
